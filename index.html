Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Weather Forecast app (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We request access to your device's location to show weather forecasts for your current area.
  • We do not collect, store, or share your personal location data on our servers. The location coordinates are used only temporarily to fetch weather data from the OpenWeather API.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your location solely to request weather forecasts from OpenWeather. The data is processed in real time and is not stored or shared by us.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the OpenWeather API to retrieve weather data. Please refer to OpenWeather’s Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Security

We do not store or process personal data on our own servers. All data transfers are secured using HTTPS.

5. Your Choices

You can deny location permission in your device settings. If location is denied, you will not receive weather data for your current position.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this file.
